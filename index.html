Hello!

I am Nayoung Lee, a fourth-year Ph.D. student in Economics at Indiana University Bloomington.

My research interests are in macroeconomics and macroeconometrics. I study how households' beliefs about fiscal policy shape macroeconomic outcomes, with a particular focus on government debt dynamics. I am also interested in macroeconomic forecasting.
